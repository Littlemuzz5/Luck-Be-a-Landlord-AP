V43 makes regular LBAL rarity chance the default.
- unlockable_chance_boost remains off by default.
- unlockable_chance_boost_mode default is now regular.
- New /boostmode regular command.
- regular mode does not focus/filter by category or rarity; it uses normal LBAL rarity chances.
- category and category_and_rarity boosting are still available if the player wants them.
