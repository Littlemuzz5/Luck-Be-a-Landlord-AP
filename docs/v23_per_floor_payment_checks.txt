V23 adds actual per-floor payment checks.

YAML:
  floor_payment_checks: per_floor

Effects:
- New seeds use Floor X Payment Y locations instead of shared Payment Y.
- The patched PCK reads current_floor directly and queues Floor X Payment Y when rent is paid.
- It also queues old Payment Y for compatibility with older/shared seeds; the client only sends locations that exist.
- lbal_run_state.json now includes floor/payment/spins/rent_due for client-side tracking.
