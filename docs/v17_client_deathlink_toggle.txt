V17 client DeathLink toggle

Adds a runtime client command:
  /deathlink status
  /deathlink on
  /deathlink off
  /deathlink toggle
  /deathlink auto

/deathlink off disables incoming DeathLinks and failed-rent DeathLink sends in the LBAL client without needing a new YAML or seed. The setting is saved in the client bridge folder client_settings.json.

/deathlink auto clears the local override and returns to the YAML/slot DeathLink option.
