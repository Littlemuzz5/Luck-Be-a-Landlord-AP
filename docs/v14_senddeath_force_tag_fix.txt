V14 senddeath force-tag fix

Fixes /senddeath not working in the Luck be a Landlord AP client.

Changes:
- /senddeath no longer refuses just because ctx.tags has not updated yet.
- /senddeath force-adds the DeathLink tag for this client session.
- /senddeath sends ConnectUpdate before the DeathLink Bounce packet.
- /senddeath sends the DeathLink Bounce with force=True, so slot_data parsing cannot block manual tests.
